Baked Tuna Ring

Description

A mass of tuna formed in a ring filled with slimy lima beans that are larger than quarters.

Ingredients

  • Two 7-oz cans of tuna fish, drained and flaked
  • 1 cup of Quaker or Mother's Oats (quick or old fashioned, uncooked)
  • 1/2 cup cooked peas
  • 2 tablespoons chopped onion
  • 2 tablespoons chopped green pepper
  • 2 tablespoons chopped green pepper
  • 2 tablespoons chopped pimiento
  • 1/4 cup slivered almonds
  • 2 teaspoons salt
  • 1/4 teaspoon pepper
  • 1 tablespoon Worcestershire sauce
  • 2 eggs, beaten
  • 1 1/4 cups of milk

Steps

  1. Heat oven to moderate (350 deg. fahrenheit)

  2. Combine all ingredients.

  3. Place ingredients in a greased aluminum foil-lined 8-inch ring mold (5 cup capacity).

  4. Bake in preheated oven (350 deg. fahrenheit) 50 minutes to 1 hour, or until set.

  5. Invert on platter; fill center with buttered baby lima beans.

Notes: Makes 6 servings of disgusting deliciousness.
