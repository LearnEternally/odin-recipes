Tomato Soup Cake

Description

This thing looks pretty nasty. Why would anyone make this?

Ingredients

  • 2 tbsp. butter
  • 3/4 cup walnuts
  • 1 cup white sugar
  • 1 can tomato soup
  • 1 tsp. baking soda
  • 1 tsp. cinnamon
  • 1 1/2 cup sifted flour
  • 1/2 tsp. cloves
  • 1 cup dates or raisins

Steps

  1. Cream butter, add sugar and egg.
  2. Dissolve the soda in a little of the soup, and add alternately with the flour and spices.
  3. Add the nuts and dates.
  4. Bake in moderate oven for one hour.

Note: Tomato Soup Cake should be made a few days before it is to be served, as it improves the flavour. - Mrs. P. Karpyshyn
